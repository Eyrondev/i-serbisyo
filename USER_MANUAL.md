## Introduction

**i-Serbisyo** is a comprehensive web-based system designed to streamline barangay services, particularly the processing and management of certificates and documents. The system digitizes the traditional paper-based process, making it more efficient, transparent, and accessible to residents.

### Key Features

- **Online Certificate Requests** - Residents can request certificates from anywhere
- **Real-time Status Tracking** - Monitor your request progress in real-time
- **Digital Payment Processing** - Secure payment tracking and receipt generation
- **Role-based Access Control** - Separate portals for Residents, Clerks, and Admins
- **Document Management** - Upload and manage required documents digitally
- **Automated Notifications** - Email notifications for status updates
- **Comprehensive Reporting** - Analytics and reports for administrators

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **Web Browser:** Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet Connection:** Stable internet connection
- **Email Address:** Valid email for account registration and notifications

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the i-Serbisyo URL (provided by your barangay)
3. You will see the login page

### Creating an Account

#### For Residents:

1. Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"** on the login page
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Username (unique)
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Full Name
3. Click **"Register"**
4. Check your email for verification (if enabled)
5. Complete your resident profile after first login

#### First-Time Login:

1. Enter your username/email and password
2. Click **"Login"**
3. You will be redirected to complete your resident profile
4. Fill in all required information:
   - Personal details (name, birth date, gender, civil status)
   - Contact information (phone, email)
   - Address (house number, street, purok)
   - Upload required documents (valid ID, proof of residency)
5. Submit for approval

## Resident Portal

### Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:
- **Quick Stats:** Pending requests, approved certificates, total payments
- **Recent Requests:** Your latest certificate requests
- **Notifications:** Important updates and reminders

### Requesting a Certificate

1. **Navigate to Certificates**
   - Click **"Certificates"** in the navigation menu
   - Or click **"Request Certificate"** button on dashboard

2. **Select Certificate Type**
   - Choose from available certificate types:
     - Barangay Clearance
     - Certificate of Residency
     - Certificate of Indigency
     - Business Permit
     - And more...

3. **Fill in Request Details**
   - **Purpose:** Select the purpose for your certificate
     - Employment
     - School Requirements
     - Business
     - Travel
     - Government Transaction
     - Other (specify)
   - **Additional Notes:** Provide any special instructions or information

4. **Review and Submit**
   - Review your information
   - Check the certificate fee
   - Click **"Submit Request"**
   - You'll receive a Request ID (e.g., REQ-123-2025)

5. **Confirmation**
   - You'll see a success message with your Request ID
   - An email notification will be sent (if configured)
   - The request will appear in your certificates list

### Tracking Your Request

1. Go to **"My Certificates"** page
2. View all your requests with their current status:
   - **Pending:** Waiting for clerk review
   - **Processing:** Being processed by clerk
   - **Approved:** Approved and ready for payment
   - **Completed:** Certificate is ready
   - **Ready:** Available for pickup/download
   - **Claimed:** You've received the certificate
   - **Rejected:** Request was denied (reason provided)

3. Click on any request to view detailed information:
   - Request date
   - Current status
   - Processing timeline
   - Payment status
   - Certificate number (when approved)
   - Download option (when ready)

### Managing Your Profile

1. Click on your **profile icon** or **"Profile"** in the menu
2. View your current information
3. Click **"Edit Profile"** to update:
   - Contact information
   - Address
   - Profile picture
   - Documents
4. Save changes

### Viewing Payment History

1. Navigate to **"Payments"** or **"Payment History"**
2. View all your payments:
   - Payment date
   - Service type
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Receipt number
   - Status
3. Click on a payment to view details or download receipt

## Clerk Portal

### Dashboard Overview

The clerk dashboard displays:
- **Pending Requests:** Certificates awaiting processing
- **Today's Statistics:** Daily processing metrics
- **Payment Summary:** Today's collections
- **Quick Actions:** Common tasks

### Processing Certificate Requests

#### Viewing Requests

1. Navigate to **"Certificate Requests"**
2. Filter requests by:
   - Status (pending, processing, approved, etc.)
   - Certificate type
   - Date range
   - Resident name
3. Click on a request to view full details

#### Approving a Request

1. Open the certificate request
2. Review resident information and documents
3. Verify all details are correct
4. Click **"Approve"** button
5. The system will:
   - Generate a certificate number
   - Update status to "approved"
   - Notify the resident
   - Create payment record

#### Rejecting a Request

1. Open the certificate request
2. Click **"Reject"** button
3. Provide a clear rejection reason
4. Confirm rejection
5. Resident will be notified with the reason

#### Completing a Certificate

1. Open an approved request
2. Click **"Mark as Completed"**
3. The system will:
   - Update status to "completed"
   - Generate payment record
   - Make it ready for payment processing

### Managing Payments

#### Payment List

1. Navigate to **"Payment List"**
2. View all payment transactions
3. Filter by:
   - Payment status
   - Payment method
   - Date range
   - Service type

#### Processing Payments

1. Open a payment record
2. Select payment method:
   - Cash
   - GCash
   - Bank Transfer
   - Online Payment
3. Enter payment details:
   - Reference number (if applicable)
   - Amount received
4. Click **"Mark as Paid"**
5. System generates receipt automatically

#### Generating Receipts

1. Receipts are auto-generated when payment is marked as paid
2. Receipt number format: RCP-YYYYMMDD-XXXX
3. Download or print receipt for resident
4. Receipt includes:
   - Receipt number
   - Payment date
   - Payer information
   - Service details
   - Amount breakdown
   - Official barangay seal/logo

### Resident Management

1. Navigate to **"Residents"**
2. View all registered residents
3. Search by name, email, or phone
4. Click on a resident to view:
   - Full profile
   - Certificate history
   - Payment history
   - Contact information
5. Update resident status if needed

## Certificate Types

### Barangay Clearance

**Purpose:** General clearance for various transactions  
**Common Uses:**
- Employment requirements
- Business applications
- Government transactions
- School enrollment

**Requirements:**
- Valid ID
- Proof of residency
- Recent photo

**Processing Time:** 1-3 business days  
**Validity:** 6 months to 1 year

---

### Certificate of Residency

**Purpose:** Proof of residence in the barangay  
**Common Uses:**
- School enrollment
- Scholarship applications
- Government benefits
- Legal documents

**Requirements:**
- Valid ID
- Proof of address
- Utility bill or lease contract

**Processing Time:** 1-2 business days  
**Validity:** 6 months

---

### Certificate of Indigency

**Purpose:** Proof of financial status  
**Common Uses:**
- Medical assistance
- Educational assistance
- Legal aid
- Government programs

**Requirements:**
- Valid ID
- Proof of income (or lack thereof)
- Barangay residency proof

**Processing Time:** 2-3 business days  
**Validity:** 3-6 months

---

### Business Permit

**Purpose:** Authorization to operate a business  
**Common Uses:**
- Starting a new business
- Renewing business operations
- Business expansion

**Requirements:**
- Valid ID
- Business registration documents
- Location sketch
- Barangay clearance

**Processing Time:** 3-5 business days  
**Validity:** 1 year (renewable)

## Payment Process

### Payment Methods

1. **Cash Payment**
   - Pay directly at barangay office
   - Receive official receipt immediately
   - Operating hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

2. **GCash**
   - Send payment to official barangay GCash number
   - Include reference number in message
   - Upload payment screenshot
   - Wait for verification

3. **Bank Transfer**
   - Transfer to official barangay bank account
   - Use certificate number as reference
   - Submit deposit slip
   - Allow 1-2 days for verification

4. **Online Payment** (if available)
   - Pay through integrated payment gateway
   - Instant confirmation
   - Automatic receipt generation

### Payment Workflow

1. **Certificate Approved**
   - You receive notification
   - Payment record is created
   - Fee amount is displayed

2. **Make Payment**
   - Choose payment method
   - Complete payment
   - Keep proof of payment

3. **Payment Verification**
   - Clerk verifies payment
   - Updates payment status
   - Generates official receipt

4. **Certificate Release**
   - Status changes to "ready"
   - Download digital copy (if available)
   - Schedule pickup for physical copy

### Payment Receipts

Official receipts include:
- Receipt number
- Date and time
- Payer information
- Service description
- Amount paid
- Payment method
- Clerk's name
- Official barangay seal

**Important:** Keep your receipt for your records and future reference.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Cannot Login

**Problem:** "Invalid username or password" error

**Solutions:**
1. Verify username and password are correct
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Try password reset:
   - Click "Forgot Password"
   - Enter your email
   - Check email for reset link
   - Create new password
4. Contact admin if account is locked

---

#### Request Not Showing

**Problem:** Submitted request doesn't appear in list

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Check internet connection
3. Verify request was submitted successfully
4. Check email for confirmation
5. Contact clerk if issue persists

---

#### Cannot Upload Documents

**Problem:** Error when uploading ID or documents

**Solutions:**
1. Check file size (maximum 5MB per file)
2. Verify file format (JPG, PNG, PDF only)
3. Ensure file is not corrupted
4. Try different browser
5. Clear browser cache
6. Compress large images before uploading

---

#### Payment Not Reflected

**Problem:** Paid but status still shows unpaid

**Solutions:**
1. Allow 1-2 hours for processing
2. Verify payment was sent to correct account
3. Check if reference number was included
4. Contact barangay office with proof of payment
5. Visit office during business hours if urgent

---

#### Certificate Not Ready

**Problem:** Status stuck on "processing" for too long

**Solutions:**
1. Check estimated processing time
2. Review if additional documents are needed
3. Contact barangay office for status update
4. Visit office if beyond normal processing time

---

#### Email Notifications Not Received

**Problem:** Not receiving status update emails

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address in profile is correct
3. Add barangay email to contacts
4. Check email settings/filters
5. Update email address if needed

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: Click "Register" on the login page, fill in your details, and submit. Complete your resident profile after first login.

**Q: Is there a fee for registration?**  
A: No, registration is free. You only pay for certificate services.

**Q: Can I request multiple certificates at once?**  
A: Yes, you can submit multiple requests. Each will be processed separately.

**Q: How long does processing take?**  
A: Processing time varies by certificate type, typically 1-5 business days.

**Q: Can I cancel a request?**  
A: Contact the barangay office immediately. Cancellation may be possible if not yet processed.

---

### Certificate Questions

**Q: What documents do I need?**  
A: Generally, you need a valid ID and proof of residency. Specific requirements vary by certificate type.

**Q: How long is a certificate valid?**  
A: Validity varies: Barangay Clearance (6-12 months), Residency (6 months), Indigency (3-6 months).

**Q: Can I get a certificate for someone else?**  
A: You can only request for yourself. For minors or dependents, contact the office for special procedures.

**Q: What if my request is rejected?**  
A: You'll receive a reason for rejection. Address the issues and submit a new request.

**Q: Can I download my certificate?**  
A: Yes, once status is "ready," you can download a digital copy from your dashboard.

---

### Payment Questions

**Q: When do I pay?**  
A: Payment is required after your certificate is approved and marked as completed.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Cash, GCash, bank transfer, and online payment (if available).

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**  
A: No, full payment is required. Contact the office for special circumstances.

**Q: What if I lose my receipt?**  
A: Contact the barangay office with your payment details. They can reissue a receipt.

**Q: Are there additional fees?**  
A: Fees are as listed. Additional charges may apply for rush processing (if available).

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Can I use my mobile phone?**  
A: Yes, the system is mobile-responsive and works on smartphones and tablets.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes, the system uses encryption and secure protocols to protect your information.

**Q: Can I access the system 24/7?**  
A: Yes, you can submit requests anytime. Processing occurs during office hours.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page and follow the reset instructions.

## Appendix

### Glossary of Terms

- **Barangay:** The smallest administrative division in the Philippines
- **Certificate:** Official document issued by the barangay
- **Clerk:** Staff member who processes certificate requests
- **Resident:** Person living within the barangay jurisdiction
- **Request ID:** Unique identifier for certificate requests
- **Receipt Number:** Unique identifier for payment transactions
- **Certificate Number:** Unique identifier for issued certificates

### Document Requirements Checklist

#### For All Certificates:
- [ ] Valid government-issued ID
- [ ] Proof of residency (utility bill, lease contract, etc.)
- [ ] Recent 2x2 photo
- [ ] Completed application form

#### Additional for Business Permit:
- [ ] Business registration documents
- [ ] Location sketch/map
- [ ] Barangay clearance
- [ ] DTI/SEC registration

#### Additional for Indigency:
- [ ] Proof of income (or affidavit of no income)
- [ ] Barangay certification
- [ ] Supporting documents for assistance purpose
